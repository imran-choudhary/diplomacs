Details of course:

The Diploma in Computer Engineering is 3 year course, which is further divided in to 6 semester, 2 semester exams per year. This course has:
1. 6 semester exams
2. 12 sessional tests
3. 23 Practical exams
4. 1 minor project
5. 1 Industrial Visit
6. Major Project
